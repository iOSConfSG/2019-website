1 Day - Single Track - 2 Topics

Speakers for Workshop

Topics:

Morning Session: 9AM - 12:30PM
Modern iOS App architectures

Designing reliable iOS apps from scratch.


Lunch will be served at 12:30 PM

Afternoon Session: 1:30PM - 5PM
Improving App Performance

Things to consider to improve the performance of your application - starting from improving the compile time to improving app start-up time, from improving the memory footprint of the application to caching. We'll try to cover as much as possible in this short session and possibly point you in the right directions to make your app highly performant and responsive.
